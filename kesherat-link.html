Kesherat-Link

The Bridge Between Agriculture and Technology

Kesherat-Link is our revolutionary platform that transforms any agricultural hardware into intelligent, coordinated AI agents. Connect your existing equipment, sensors, and systems into one unified network that works together seamlessly.

What is Kesherat-Link?

Kesherat-Link is the bridge that connects your farm's hardware and software to our AI platform, transforming them into intelligent agents. It doesn't just enable communication - it converts any device, from legacy tractors to modern IoT sensors, into autonomous AI agents that can think, coordinate, and optimize your farm operations. Think of it as the technology that gives your equipment intelligence and the ability to work together seamlessly.

Key Features

Hardware Agnostic

Works with any brand, any model, any age. From John Deere to custom-built sensors, if it has data, we can connect it.

Real-Time Integration

Instant data synchronization across all your devices. No delays, no manual uploads, just seamless connectivity.

Secure Communication

Enterprise-grade encryption ensures your farm data stays private and protected at all times.

Easy Installation

Plug-and-play adapters for most equipment. Professional installation support available for complex setups.

Scalable Architecture

Start with one device or connect hundreds. The system grows with your farm's needs.

API Access

Full API access for custom integrations and third-party applications.

How It Works

  • Step 1: Send your device data to our MQTT portals or REST APIs - we support standard protocols for easy integration
  • Step 2: Your devices automatically register with the Kesherat platform
  • Step 3: AI agents are created for each device based on its capabilities and data streams
  • Step 4: Start commanding your farm through natural language in AgroMonitor
  • Step 5: Watch as your equipment coordinates autonomously to optimize operations

Need help getting started? Contact us for detailed integration documentation, MQTT broker details, API endpoints, and technical support. We'll guide you through the entire setup process.
