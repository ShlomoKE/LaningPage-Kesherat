About Kesherat

Paving the way into agentic agriculture

We're on a mission to revolutionize agriculture by bridging the gap between traditional farming and cutting-edge AI technology. Kesherat empowers farmers to transform their operations into intelligent, autonomous systems.

Kesherat was born from a simple observation: modern farms are drowning in technology that doesn't talk to each other. Farmers invest in sensors, machinery, and software, but spend more time managing these tools than actually farming. We knew there had to be a better way.


Our founders, with backgrounds in AI, agriculture, and systems engineering, came together to create a platform that would finally make agricultural technology work for farmers, not against them. The result is Kesherat - a platform that turns any farm equipment into intelligent agents that coordinate autonomously.
